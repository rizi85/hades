
# Social Media Phishing

## Description

Social Media Phishing is a cyber attack where phishers use social media platforms to trick users into providing sensitive information or downloading malware.

## Steps to identify

1. Suspicious messages or posts asking for personal information.
2. Unusual links from unknown or familiar contacts urging immediate action.
3. Requests to download or share content that seems out of character.

Since detailed specific examples and sources for Social Media Phishing were not provided directly, these steps are based on common characteristics of phishing attacks.
